Resume

Esports has always been a big part of my life. I enjoy watching it and I enjoy playing it. I professionally play Super Smash Bros. Ultimate both at the collegiate level and the state level. Below is a history of some important moments while being invovled in Esports that I wanted to make known.

GAME: Super Smash Bros Ultimate
TAG: leiadog
MAIN: King Dedede

  • April 21st 2018

    BSU Paid Bracket

    I entered my first ever Smash 4 bracket with my main King Dedede. The bracket was held at Boise State in the Jordan Ballroom. I played against who is now the #1 best player in Boise. This got me started in the tournament scene.

    https://gss.challonge.com/bc5xg7nt

  • June 22nd 2019

    ABU 37

    This was my first ever Smash Ultimate bracket at ABU Games. This was a scary first step but after it was taken, I knew where the tournaments were being hosted so I could continue to participate.

    https://gss.challonge.com/06222019ABU37SUS

  • August 17th 2019

    ABU 40

    This was my best showing at a tournament yet. I beat a PR (Power Ranking) player by the tag name of Rooster. This is what really made me realize I love competing and that this tournament wouldn't be my last.

    https://gss.challonge.com/081719ABU40SUS

  • January 30th 2022

    Sunday Scrap #1

    I hosted my first every tournament. I took a break from competing during the pandemic and jumped back into it 2021. By 2022, I had gained support from the community and started hosting my own tournament once a month on Sundays.

    https://www.start.gg/tournament/the-sunday-scrap-1/events

  • April 9th 2022

    Beak Bonk 2022

    I traveled out of state to my first every Regional in Pullman, WA. This was the biggest tournament I had attended at the time.


    https://www.start.gg/tournament/beak-bonk-2022-1/details

  • June 24th 2022

    CEO 2022

    I flew all the way to Florida for what is known as a Super Major. It was my first Super Major and it was a huge tournament. Having almost no sleep from the flight, I felt I could've played better, but it was an awesome experience regardless.


    https://www.start.gg/tournament/ceo-2022-fighting-game-championships/details

  • October 15th 2022

    Back in Blood 5

    Another out of state regional that was in Portland, Oregon. This is the second rendition of this tournament that I went too. My first one I went 1-2. However this time, I played great and was very proud of my placing. The PNW began to know my name after this tournament, saying that I may be underseeded at these big events.


    https://www.start.gg/tournament/back-in-blood-5/details

  • May 13th 2023

    Good Manners and Customs

    Upcoming: I currently have the privellege to host my own regional here in Boise in May. Hopefully it will be a big event and it is going to be on Boise State's campus. I'm very excited for this event.

    https://www.start.gg/tournament/good-manners-and-customs-gmc/details
